About Us

Nacheral Flooring is established on 2019 and manged by Haote Contracting Sdn Bhd. Nacheral Flooring is specializing in supply and install SPC Vinyl flooring, 12mm Laminate Flooring, Wallboard, Decking and Skirting in Malaysia.

Nacheral Flooring was a company that inspired by natural. Therefore, it advocates the whole new material for home improvement revolution that have the following advantages:

  • Moisturing & Thermal Insulation
  • Sound Prevention
  • Fire Prevention
  • Superhard Strength
  • Waterproof
  • Fast Installation
  • Environmental
  • Non Deformation
  • Save Space
  • Multifunctional Installation Method
